## polix

A DSL for writing declarative policies with document accessors and URI support.

### Overview

polix provides a domain-specific language (DSL) for writing declarative policies in Clojure. It includes document accessors and URI support, making it easier to define and enforce policies across your application.

### Features

* Declarative policy DSL
* Document accessor support
* URI-based policy definitions
* Flexible policy enforcement
