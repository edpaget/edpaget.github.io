## yprosemirror

Bidirectional sync between ProseMirror editors and Y-CRDT for real-time collaboration.

### Overview

yprosemirror enables synchronization between ProseMirror editors and Y-CRDT, providing bidirectional sync capabilities for building real-time collaborative editing experiences within the ProseMirror framework.

### Features

* Seamless integration between ProseMirror and Y-CRDT
* Bidirectional synchronization
* Real-time collaborative editing
* Conflict-free merging of concurrent edits
